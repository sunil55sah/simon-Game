Simon Game Notes

  • Objective: The goal of the Simon game is to test and improve your memory skills by remembering and repeating sequences of colors.
  • Getting Started: Press any key on your keyboard to start the game. Follow the on-screen instructions to play.
  • Gameplay:
    • Pay attention to the sequence of colors displayed by the game.
    • Click or tap the colored buttons to repeat the sequence.
    • The game will progress to the next level if you correctly repeat the sequence.
    • Keep track of your progress and aim to reach higher levels.
  • Game Over:
    • If you make a mistake and repeat the sequence incorrectly, the game will end.
    • You can restart the game by pressing any key on your keyboard.
  • Controls:
    • Keyboard: Use any key to start the game and restart after game over.
    • Mouse or Touch: Click or tap the colored buttons to repeat the sequence.
  • Tips:
    • Focus and concentrate to remember the sequence accurately.
    • Start with shorter sequences and gradually increase the difficulty.
    • Challenge yourself to beat your high score and reach higher levels.
  • Have Fun! Enjoy playing the Simon game and challenge yourself to improve your memory skills!
